Smart Eye Mouse

The Smart Eye Mouse is a hands-free device that uses eye-tracking technology to control the computer cursor. By tracking eye movements, users can navigate and interact with the screen effortlessly. It is particularly beneficial for individuals with mobility impairments, offering them greater independence and accessibility. The mouse is customization, allowing users to adjust sensitivity and click settings. Compatible with various devices and operating systems, it revolutionizes human-computer interaction by enabling seamless, hands-free control and enhancing productivity for users of all abilities.
